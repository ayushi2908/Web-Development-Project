Introduction

Bhaktivedanta Club is lead by Prof. Laxmidhar Behera at IIT Kanpur. Under guidance of sir, many people work. Main goal of this club is to spread knowledge of Bhagvad Gita. We celebarte many festivals, perform sankirtan at different places, go on retreats to holy places and perform many activities with Lord Krishna in center.

Harinaam Sankirtan at Nankari and Gooba Garden

Harinaam Sankirtan at Nankari and Gooba Garden; IITK lead by Her Grace Gopali mataji and Dr. Suvendu Samanta prabhuji. Followed by free Prasad distribution. Around 80 people join every Sunday in this preaching program.

About Us

We spread the knowledge of Bhagvad Gita. We do spiritual activities according to instructions from Vedic Scriptures.Bhaktivedanta Club is a part of Hare Krishna Movement started by Srila Prabhupada. To join our club please do register.
